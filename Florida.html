Florida - The Sunshine State

Culture

Florida has a diverse culture influenced by Latin American, Caribbean, and Southern traditions. Cities like Miami are known for their vibrant art scene, Cuban influence, and colorful festivals such as Calle Ocho.

Food

Florida offers a mix of seafood, Cuban, and Southern cuisine. Some must-try dishes include:

  • Key Lime Pie - A famous dessert from the Florida Keys.
  • Cuban Sandwich - A delicious mix of ham, pork, Swiss cheese, and pickles.
  • Stone Crab - A Florida delicacy, best enjoyed in season.
  • Gator Bites - Fried alligator meat, popular in the Everglades region.

Accommodation

Florida has a variety of accommodation options:

  • Luxury Resorts - The Ritz-Carlton, Four Seasons, and Fontainebleau Miami Beach.
  • Budget Hotels - Comfort Inn, La Quinta, and Motel 6.
  • Airbnb & Rentals - Beachfront condos, cabins, and vacation homes.
  • Camping - State parks and RV sites for nature lovers.

Tourist Attractions

Florida is full of amazing places to visit:

  • Disney World - The world's most famous theme park.
  • Miami Beach - Beautiful beaches and exciting nightlife.
  • Everglades National Park - Home to alligators and unique wildlife.
  • Key West - A tropical paradise at the southernmost point of the US.
  • Universal Studios - A must-visit for movie and roller coaster fans.
